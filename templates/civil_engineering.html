Civil Engineering Projects

Below are some of the projects I have worked on, where I was responsible for the reinforced concrete engineering aspects as a civil engineer.

Köln Radsportzentrum – A cycling arena built to modern standards for both professional events and community use.

Grand Tower – The Grand Tower in Frankfurt, stands at 180 meters and is Germany's tallest residential building, featuring 47 floors and 413.

Stuttgart Apartments – The Stuttgart Residences, designed by Richard Meier & Partners, is a six-level condominium building situated in northern Stuttgart, featuring expansive glass facades, white aluminum panels, and terraces that offer panoramic views over the city.

Amprion – This sustainably designed four-story building by Amprion in Dortmund’s Phoenix-West Technology Park features green courtyards, geothermal energy, and rooftop solar panels, setting a new standard for modern, eco-friendly workplaces.

Dock 14 – Dock 14 in Münster is a seven-story office building at Am Mittelhafen, showcasing advanced civil engineering through its dual-structure design—combining a thermally efficient clinker brick volume and a transparent glass section—while offering over 10,000 m² of modern workspace and robust underground infrastructure.

Messe Düsseldorf – Messe Düsseldorf, located on Stockumer Kirchstraße, is one of Europe's largest and most advanced exhibition centres, featuring 18 state-of-the-art halls spread over approximately 305,000 m², designed with ground-level access and a concentric layout to facilitate efficient visitor flow and large-scale event logistics.

The Klimaschutzsiedlung "Am Wald" – The Klimaschutzsiedlung "Am Wald" in Düsseldorf-Benrath is a multi-generational residential development comprising 62 barrier-free apartments built to passive house standards, featuring interconnected structures with recessed, glazed stairwells that create a noise-protected central courtyard and integrate solar thermal systems and gas condensing boilers for sustainable energy efficiency.

Vitanas Senioren Centrum – The Vitanas Senioren Centrum Am Kulturpark in Neubrandenburg is a senior care facility designed to provide comfortable, age-appropriate living and support services for elderly residents in a modern, community-oriented environment.

Grafental WA6 – Grafental WA6 in Düsseldorf is a sustainable residential development featuring energy-efficient buildings with varied facades and modern civil engineering techniques that create a vibrant, durable urban living environment.

Klinikum Veterinärmedizin – The Klinikum Veterinärmedizin at Justus-Liebig-Universität Gießen is a modern veterinary clinic featuring sustainable design and reinforced concrete construction that supports flexible, efficient clinical and research spaces.
