About Me

Hi, my name is Réka Gábosi. I’m an aspiring AI / Machine Learning Engineer with a background in civil engineering and project management, currently transitioning into building intelligent, scalable, and secure AI systems using Python, machine learning, and deep learning frameworks.

Since December 2024, I have been completing advanced AI-focused programs, including IBM’s AI Engineering, IBM’s Generative AI Engineering, and Harvard University’s CS50’s Introduction to Artificial Intelligence with Python. Through these programs, I gained hands-on experience developing and deploying real-world projects involving machine learning pipelines, deep learning models, NLP applications, RAG systems, computer vision, and generative AI.

My technical toolkit includes Python, TensorFlow, Keras, PyTorch, NumPy, Pandas, Flask, and SQLite, with a strong focus on building end-to-end AI solutions — from data processing and model training to deployment and inference. Many of these projects are publicly available on my GitHub.

To support production-ready AI systems, I have also completed the Packt Docker and Kubernetes Masterclass: From Beginner to Advanced. This strengthened my ability to containerize machine learning applications, manage dependencies, and deploy scalable AI services using Docker and Kubernetes — skills essential for modern MLOps and real-world AI deployment.

Cybersecurity is an important complementary focus in my work, particularly in the context of securing AI models and ML systems. I completed AI Security: Risks, Defences and Safety with Macquarie University, where I explored topics such as adversarial threats, model vulnerabilities, data leakage, and responsible AI deployment. This has shaped how I think about building AI systems that are not only effective, but also robust, secure, and trustworthy.

While my professional background is rooted in civil engineering, it has given me a strong foundation in structured problem-solving, system-level thinking, and managing complex projects. These skills translate naturally into AI engineering, where scalability, reliability, and long-term impact are just as critical as model performance.

I am actively seeking opportunities to transition into an AI / Machine Learning Engineering role. I am motivated to join a team where I can contribute to building intelligent systems, grow as an engineer, and work on meaningful, future-focused technologies.
