About Me

Hi, my name is Réka Gábosi. I’m a civil engineer and currently working as a project manager, but I’m also on a journey to become an AI programmer, with a growing interest in cybersecurity.

I chose civil engineering because I’ve always been drawn to the idea of building something real — something I could walk past years later and know I helped bring to life. There’s a special kind of pride in knowing that your work might stand for decades, maybe even centuries.

My interest in programming started with a fascination for technology. As AI began advancing rapidly, I found myself wanting to understand how it really works — what’s under the hood — and that curiosity quickly turned into a passion.

Cybersecurity became a natural extension of that journey. With AI evolving, so do the risks. I want to be part of the effort to protect people from digital threats — and to better protect myself and others from scams, data loss, and misinformation.

Whether I’m designing a building or writing code, it’s the same mindset — structure, logic, and a drive to build things that last.
