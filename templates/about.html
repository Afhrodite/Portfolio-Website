About Me

Hi, my name is Réka Gábosi. I’m currently working as a project manager in civil engineering, but I’m transitioning my career into AI engineering — building intelligent systems with Python, machine learning, and deep learning frameworks.

Since December 2024, I have been completing advanced AI programs, including IBM’s AI Engineering, IBM’s Generative AI Engineering, and Harvard University’s CS50’s Introduction to Artificial Intelligence with Python. These programs gave me hands-on experience with Python, TensorFlow, Keras, PyTorch, NumPy, Pandas, Flask, and SQLite — developing and deploying projects in NLP, RAG systems, computer vision, and generative AI, many of which I’ve shared on GitHub.

While my professional background is in civil engineering, the skills I’ve built there — structured problem-solving, designing systems that last, and leading multidisciplinary projects — translate directly into AI engineering. Whether managing complex construction projects or building scalable AI solutions, I approach challenges with logic, structure, and long-term impact in mind.

Cybersecurity has also become a side focus of mine. With AI evolving, so do its risks, which is why I completed AI Security: Risks, Defences and Safety with Macquarie University. It strengthened my understanding of how to build and deploy AI systems responsibly and securely.

I am actively seeking opportunities to transition into AI engineering. I’m especially motivated to join a team where I can apply my technical skills, continue growing as an engineer, and contribute to meaningful, future-driven projects.
